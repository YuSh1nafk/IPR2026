Tom: Hi, Trang. Surprised to see you. What brings you here? Trang: Oh, hello Tom. I'm looking for a knitting kit. Tom: A knitting kit? I didn’t know you like knitting. Trang: Actually, I'm keen on many DIY activities. In my leisure time, I love knitting, building dollhouses, and making paper flowers. Tom: I see. So, you like spending time on your own. Trang: Yeah. What do you do in your free time? Tom: I'm a bit different. I usually hang out with my friends. We go to the cinema, go cycling, or play sport in the park. Trang: You love spending free time with other people, don’t you? Tom: That’s right. By the way, would you like to go to the cinema with me and Mark this Sunday? There’s a new comedy at New World Cinema. Trang: Yes, I’d love to. Can I ask Mai to join us? Tom: Sure. Let’s meet outside the cinema at 9 a.m.
