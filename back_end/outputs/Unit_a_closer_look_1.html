Leisure Activities

Lesson: A CLOSER LOOK 1

I. OBJECTIVES

  • Students will be able to use vocabulary related to likes and dislikes.
  • Students will practice using expressions of likes and dislikes in sentences.
  • Students will differentiate between the sounds /ʊ/ and /u:/ in English pronunciation.

II. MATERIALS

  • Textbook
  • Audio player
  • Board and markers
  • Worksheets for vocabulary exercises
  • Pronunciation charts

III. PROCEDURES

Warm-up (5 minutes)
  • Aim: To introduce the topic of likes and dislikes.
  • Content: The teacher will introduce the topic by asking students about their favorite activities.
  • Target Language/Details: Likes, dislikes
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Ask students about their favorite activities and write their responses on the board.
Students Respond to the teacher's questions about their favorite activities.
Stage 1: Vocabulary Matching (10 minutes)
  • Aim: To introduce and practice expressions about likes.
  • Content: Students will match words/phrases in column A with words in column B to make expressions about likes.
  • Target Language/Details: Taking photos, Interested in, History, Keen on, Crazy about, Fond of, Surfing the net, DIY
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Distribute worksheets with columns A and B for matching exercises.
Students Work individually to match words/phrases from column A with column B.
Teacher Review the answers with the class and provide correct matches.
Stage 2: Fill in the Blanks (15 minutes)
  • Aim: To practice using vocabulary in context through sentence completion.
  • Content: Students will fill in the blanks in sentences using words from the box.
  • Target Language/Details: Fond of, Interested in, Keen on, Not into, Crazy about
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Provide students with sentences that have blanks to fill in using words from a given box.
Students Complete the sentences individually using the correct words from the box.
Teacher Go over the completed sentences with the class and discuss any mistakes.
Stage 3: Sentence Completion (10 minutes)
  • Aim: To allow students to express their own likes and dislikes using given phrases.
  • Content: Students will complete sentences about their own likes and dislikes using phrases from the box or their own ideas.
  • Target Language/Details: Crazy about, Keen on, Fond of, Not interested in, Not into
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Ask students to complete sentences using phrases from the box or their own ideas.
Students Write sentences about their likes and dislikes using the provided phrases.
Teacher Invite students to share their sentences with the class.
Stage 4: Pronunciation Practice - /ʊ/ and /u:/ (10 minutes)
  • Aim: To practice pronunciation of the sounds /ʊ/ and /u:/ in English.
  • Content: Students will listen and repeat words, focusing on the sounds /ʊ/ and /u:/, and categorize them.
  • Target Language/Details: /ʊ/, /u:/
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Play audio of words containing /ʊ/ and /u:/ and ask students to repeat.
Students Listen to the audio and repeat the words, focusing on pronunciation.
Teacher Ask students to categorize the words into the correct columns based on their sounds.
Students Sort the words into the correct columns for /ʊ/ and /u:/ sounds.
Stage 5: Sentence Pronunciation Practice (15 minutes)
  • Aim: To practice identifying and pronouncing /ʊ/ and /u:/ in sentences.
  • Content: Students will listen to sentences, identify words with /ʊ/ and /u:/, and practice pronunciation.
  • Target Language/Details: Underline words with /ʊ/, Circle words with /u:/
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Play audio of sentences and ask students to underline /ʊ/ words and circle /u:/ words.
Students Listen to the sentences and mark the words with /ʊ/ and /u:/ as instructed.
Teacher Review the correct answers with the class and practice pronunciation.
Wrap-up (5 minutes)
  • Aim: To review key vocabulary and pronunciation from the lesson.
  • Content: The teacher will review the key vocabulary and sounds learned in the lesson.
  • Target Language/Details: Likes, dislikes, /ʊ/, /u:/
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Ask students to share one new thing they learned today about vocabulary or pronunciation.
Students Share what they learned about vocabulary or pronunciation during the lesson.
