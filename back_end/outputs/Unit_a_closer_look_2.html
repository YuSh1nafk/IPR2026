Leisure Time

Lesson: A CLOSER LOOK 2

I. OBJECTIVES

  • Students will be able to use verbs of liking and disliking followed by gerunds and to-infinitives.
  • Students will be able to choose the correct form of verbs in sentences.
  • Students will practice asking and answering questions about likes and dislikes using the correct verb forms.

II. MATERIALS

  • Textbook
  • Board and markers
  • Worksheets with multiple-choice questions
  • Cards with leisure activities for the mime game

III. PROCEDURES

Warm-up (5 minutes)
  • Aim: To introduce the topic of verbs of liking and disliking.
  • Content: The teacher will introduce verbs of liking and disliking by discussing favorite and least favorite activities.
  • Target Language/Details: Verbs of liking, Verbs of disliking
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Ask students about their favorite and least favorite activities and write examples on the board.
Students Respond to the teacher's questions about their favorite and least favorite activities.
Stage 1: Choose the Correct Answer (15 minutes)
  • Aim: To practice using verbs of liking and disliking with gerunds and to-infinitives.
  • Content: Students will choose the correct answer from multiple-choice questions to complete sentences.
  • Target Language/Details: Travelling, Doing, To watch, Going, Hunting
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Distribute worksheets with multiple-choice questions.
Students Individually choose the correct answer (A, B, or C) for each sentence.
Teacher Review the answers with the class, discussing why each choice is correct or incorrect.
Stage 2: Complete the Sentences (10 minutes)
  • Aim: To personalize the use of verbs of liking and disliking by completing sentences.
  • Content: Students will complete sentences about themselves using verbs of liking and disliking.
  • Target Language/Details: Verbs of liking, Verbs of disliking
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Ask students to write sentences about themselves using verbs of liking and disliking.
Students Complete the sentences individually, using verbs of liking and disliking to describe their preferences.
Teacher Invite a few students to share their sentences with the class.
Stage 3: Game - Likes and Dislikes Mimes (15 minutes)
  • Aim: To practice using verbs of liking and disliking in a fun and interactive way.
  • Content: Students will play a mime game to guess leisure activities using Yes/No questions.
  • Target Language/Details: Do you like...? Do you love...?
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Explain the rules of the mime game and divide students into groups.
Students One student mimes a leisure activity, while others guess by asking Yes/No questions using verbs of liking and disliking.
Teacher Monitor the activity and assist students with forming questions if needed.
Wrap-up (5 minutes)
  • Aim: To review the key grammar points and vocabulary from the lesson.
  • Content: The teacher will review the key grammar points and vocabulary from the lesson.
  • Target Language/Details: Verbs of liking, Verbs of disliking, Gerunds, To-infinitives
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Ask students to share one new thing they learned about using verbs of liking and disliking.
Students Share what they learned about using verbs of liking and disliking during the lesson.
