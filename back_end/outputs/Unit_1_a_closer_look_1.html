Unit 1: Leisure Activities

Lesson: A Closer Look 1

I. OBJECTIVES

  • To enhance students' vocabulary related to leisure activities.
  • To improve students' ability to use vocabulary in context.
  • To develop pronunciation skills through listening and repetition.

II. MATERIALS

  • Audio recording for pronunciation task
  • Textbook
  • Whiteboard
  • Markers

III. PROCEDURES

Warm-up (5 minutes)
  • Aim: To introduce the lesson topic and activate prior knowledge.
  • Content: Introduction to vocabulary related to leisure activities.
  • Target Language/Details: Leisure activities vocabulary
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Start the class by asking students about different leisure activities they know. Write some examples on the board.
Students Participate in the discussion by mentioning leisure activities they know.
Stage 1: Vocabulary Matching (15 minutes)
  • Aim: To familiarize students with new vocabulary and their meanings.
  • Content: Match a word/phrase in column A with its definition or synonym in column B.
  • Target Language/Details: Words/phrases from column A and B
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Explain the matching activity and instruct students to work individually to match words/phrases from column A with the correct definitions or synonyms in column B.
Students Individually match the words/phrases with their definitions or synonyms.
Teacher Review the answers with the class and provide feedback.
Stage 2: Vocabulary in Context (15 minutes)
  • Aim: To practice using vocabulary in context by filling in blanks.
  • Content: Fill in each blank with a correct word from the list provided.
  • Target Language/Details: Correct word usage
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Ask students to read the sentences and fill in each blank with the correct word from the list provided.
Students Complete the sentences by filling in the blanks with the appropriate words.
Teacher Check the answers with the class and discuss any difficulties.
Stage 3: Pronunciation Practice (10 minutes)
  • Aim: To develop correct pronunciation of the target vocabulary.
  • Content: Listen and repeat the words as pronounced in the audio.
  • Target Language/Details: Words to be listened to and repeated
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Play the audio recording and ask students to listen carefully to the pronunciation of each word.
Students Listen to the audio and repeat each word after it is played.
Teacher Monitor pronunciation and provide corrective feedback as necessary.
Wrap-up (5 minutes)
  • Aim: To consolidate the lesson's learning objectives.
  • Content: Review of vocabulary and pronunciation covered in the lesson.
  • Target Language/Details: Revised vocabulary and pronunciation
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Summarize the key points of the lesson and ask students to share one new word they learned today.
Students Reflect on the lesson and share a new word they learned.
