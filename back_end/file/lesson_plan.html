Lesson Plan: Leisure Activities

I. OBJECTIVES

  • Knowledge: Students will be able to use gerunds (V-ing) and infinitives to talk about leisure activities.
  • Core competence: Develop communication skills, collaborative skills, and language awareness.
  • Personal qualities: Develop self-study skills, confidence in expressing personal interests.

II. MATERIALS

- Grade 8 textbook
- Projector/ TV/ pictures

III. PROCEDURES

1. WARM-UP (5 mins)

Teacher’s and Ss’ activitiesContent
- T asks: "What do you like doing in your free time?"
- Ss respond quickly.
- T writes some responses on the board.		Brainstorming: "What do you do in your free time?"

2. PRESENTATION (5 mins)

Teacher’s and Ss’ activitiesContent
- T introduces the grammar point.
- T shows examples: "I like reading books" vs "I would like to read this book".
- Ss analyze the difference.		Grammar Focus: Gerunds (V-ing) and Infinitives (to V).

3. PRACTICE (8 mins)

Task 1: Identifying leisure activities

Teacher’s and Ss’ activitiesContent
- T guides students to look at the textbook.
- Ss work individually to complete sentences.
- T checks and corrects.

Task 2: Reading Comprehension

Teacher’s and Ss’ activitiesContent
- T asks students to read the text.
- Ss choose the correct answers based on the text.		2 Read about Trang's leisure activities. Choose the correct answer.

4. PRODUCTION (10 mins)

Interview a partner about their free time (Students work in pairs).

5. WRAP-UP (3 mins)

Teacher’s and Ss’ activitiesContent
- T asks: "What have we learned today?"
- Ss summarize the usage of gerunds and infinitives.		Review of Gerunds vs Infinitives.

6. HOMEWORK (1 min)

Teacher’s and Ss’ activitiesContent
- T assigns exercises from the workbook.Complete exercises on page 17 of the textbook.
