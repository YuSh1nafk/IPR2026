Leisure Time

Lesson: LOOKING BACK

I. OBJECTIVES

  • Students will be able to use vocabulary related to leisure activities in context.
  • Students will practice forming complete sentences from given cues.
  • Students will apply grammar rules for using gerunds after verbs of liking/disliking.
  • Students will enhance their writing skills by completing a passage using correct verb forms.

II. MATERIALS

  • Textbook
  • Board and markers
  • Worksheets with sentence completion and verb form exercises
  • Pictures related to leisure activities

III. PROCEDURES

Warm-up (5 minutes)
  • Aim: To engage students and introduce the topic of leisure activities.
  • Content: The teacher will introduce the topic by discussing common leisure activities and their benefits.
  • Target Language/Details: Leisure activities, Puzzles, DIY
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Ask students about their favorite leisure activities and why they enjoy them.
Students Share their favorite leisure activities and discuss the benefits with the class.
Stage 1: Complete the Sentences with Leisure Activities (10 minutes)
  • Aim: To practice using leisure activity vocabulary in context.
  • Content: Students will complete sentences with appropriate leisure activities.
  • Target Language/Details: Puzzles, DIY, Messaging, Playing football, Internet
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Provide students with sentences to complete using appropriate leisure activities.
Students Complete the sentences individually using the correct leisure activity vocabulary.
Teacher Review the completed sentences with the class and provide feedback.
Stage 2: Write Complete Sentences from Cues (15 minutes)
  • Aim: To practice forming complete sentences using given cues.
  • Content: Students will write complete sentences from given cues.
  • Target Language/Details: Crazy about, Interested in, Not fond of, Not into, Keen on
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Provide students with cues to form complete sentences.
Students Write sentences individually using the cues provided.
Teacher Ask students to share their sentences with the class and discuss any errors.
Stage 3: Fill in the Blanks with Correct Verb Forms (15 minutes)
  • Aim: To practice using gerunds after verbs of liking/disliking and correct verb forms.
  • Content: Students will fill in blanks with the correct form(s) of the verb in brackets.
  • Target Language/Details: Enjoy cycling, Like reading, Detests playing, Love chatting, Prefer doing
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Provide students with sentences containing blanks to fill in with correct verb forms.
Students Fill in the blanks individually using the correct form of the verbs in brackets.
Teacher Go over the answers with the class and explain any mistakes.
Stage 4: Complete the Passage (20 minutes)
  • Aim: To enhance writing skills by completing a passage using correct verb forms and additional words.
  • Content: Students will complete a passage using correct verb forms and pictures as prompts.
  • Target Language/Details: Use correct verb forms and add words as necessary
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Provide students with a passage to complete using correct verb forms and pictures as prompts.
Students Complete the passage individually, adding words as necessary to make it coherent.
Teacher Collect the completed passages and provide feedback on their use of language and coherence.
Wrap-up (5 minutes)
  • Aim: To review the key vocabulary and grammar points from the lesson.
  • Content: The teacher will review the key vocabulary and grammar points learned in the lesson.
  • Target Language/Details: Leisure activities, Gerunds, Verbs of liking/disliking
Role Activities (Step-by-step)
Teacher Ask students to share one new thing they learned about leisure activities or grammar today.
Students Reflect on the lesson and share their thoughts with the class.
